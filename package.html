PACKAGE SUBSTATION

Package substations are the most preferred solutions nowadays, as there are concerns regarding space, aesthetics, and safety.

ELSEWEDY ELECTRIC Package substation is a compact enclosure consisting of MV switchgear, a transformer, and an LV switchboard located in three separate/Open compartments which are segregated from each other by means of partitions in order to ensure personnel safety.

Accessibility to operate or maintain the equipment is through lockable doors provided for each compartment to maximize security.

Assembly of the complete substation is factory ready to minimize site installation time and cost.
All equipment is of high quality and tested as a complete unit.

ELSEWEDY ELECTRIC package substations are tailored to individual customer requirements and divided into compartments, one for the MV switchgear, distribution transformer, and LV switchboard.
Types:

1. Distribution substation up to 36 KV, 3 MVA
2. Primary substation 33/11 KV, 20 MVA
3. MV Switchgear compartment:
    Freestanding SF6 RMU, Extensible and non-extensible.
4. Transformer compartment:
    Transformers from 200kVA to 3 MVA up to 36 KV can be oil insulated or dry type.
5. Low Voltage compartment:
    Can use ACB or MCCB or feeder pillars– up to 5000A

Applications

Renewables and distributed generation:
package Substation suitable for harsh environment with robust design to offer different layouts as per equipment and application requirements.

Industry:
Reliable supply, improved operation efficiency and power quality are the main criteria for power supply for industries.

Utility:
The existing grids are under pressure to deliver the growing demand for power, as well as provide a stable and sustainable supply of electricity through radial and ring connections with alternative supply routes with a type tested solution with remote monitoring and switching for equipment protection and selectivity to fulfill the needs in distribution networks.

Infrastructure:
As the demand for safe and reliable electricity increases, the package substation continues to evolve and innovate in order to accommodate such growth.


Features:
- High level of safety for equipment and personnel.
- No access to live parts.
- All equipment inside the package substation is type tested as per their relevant standard.
- Can be lifted with the transformer installed.
- Designed with natural cooling and smooth airflow.
- The locking system for all doors prevents unauthorized entry of personnel.
- Stainless steel hinges for corrosion resistance.
- The enclosure has a compartmented design and  the operation activities are done from outside the enclosure.
